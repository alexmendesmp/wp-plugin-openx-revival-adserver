DCCODE OpenX Revival Adserver makes it easy to show OpenX/Revive ads on your Wordpress Site!

== Description ==

With this plugin and a little of informations about your Openx/Revive Ads you will be able to show them all on your wordpress website!

 * It wasn't so easy to show your ads ever!

== Installation ==

1. Upload the folder 'dccode-adserver-openx' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I know my OpenX/Rivave AdServer current version? =

1. Log in your Openx/Revive account. 
2. Switch to Administrator Account.
3. Go to Configuration -> Product Updates

= Affiliate ID: How to find it? =

1. Log in your Openx/Revive account.
2. Go to Inventory -> Websites -> and click on the name of the website you are working with.
3. Search for (affiliateid=) in your Browser Address Bar.

= Zone ID: How to find it? =

1. Log in your Openx/Revive account.
2. Go to Inventory -> Websites -> Zones -> and click on the name of the zone.
3. Search for (zoneid=) in your Browser Address Bar.
